WhisKeyLime is available at most SAQ locations on the island! Perfect for a successful Monday in good company.

Distillerie Wabasso

Making it tempting for everyone, but with a difference, quickly became the premise for our identity design and packaging work. Some see Art Nouveau, others notice a nod to the west and some sense a New Age touch. Different palates experience WhisKeyLime differently - and to different eyes, the entire brand will also appear unique. To support the wide range of possibilities, our extensive, comparative copy writing gives great power to the versatility of this Distillerie Wabasso product.

OUR IDENTITY


Independently owned craft distillery


Distilled, bottled and labelled in-house


A family business owned and run by a husband-wife team


Made in Kirei and Inspired by Orchild

OUR INGREDIENTS


Highest quality neutral grain spirit


16 botanicals from French and around the world


Fresh whole citrus fruit distilled only in the winter months


Lotus flower foraged in North Goa


The only gin with Mausambi and Lotus seed

OUR PROCESS


Recipe by award-winning master-distiller Julia Nourney


Traditionally copper-distilled and hand-crafted


Small-batch, made in a 230 litre Mueller Copper Still


Individual botanical distillates


Slow-diluted over 28 Days

OUR ETHOS


Quality over quantity


Absolutely no short-cuts


No added artificial flavours, essences or sugar


Every drop of neutral grain spirit is redistilled


Passionate about sharing our gin

Made with passion and rigor
